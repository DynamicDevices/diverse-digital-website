## Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive review and analysis of the Diverse Digital website, including technical performance audits, SEO analysis, user experience evaluation, and strategic recommendations. The review was conducted to support Dionne's business relaunch and identify areas for improvement.

## Key Findings Summary

### Critical Issues Identified

1. **SEO Crisis - Title Tag & Meta Description** ❌ **URGENT**
   - **Current:** "Digital Services for Faith Groups - Affordable Solutions"
   - **Should Be:** "Diverse Digital | Digital Marketing for Purpose-Driven Businesses"
   - **Status:** Still unfixed as of Nov 16, 2024
   - **Impact:** SEO score 80/100 (improved from 8/100, but could be 90+)
   - **Fix Time:** 10 minutes

2. **Missing Social Proof** ⚠️
   - No client testimonials visible
   - No case studies/portfolio
   - **Impact:** Lower trust and conversion rates

3. **Limited Content Marketing** ⚠️
   - No blog section
   - No downloadable resources
   - **Impact:** Missing SEO opportunities

### Strengths Identified

1. **Excellent Performance** ✅
   - Performance Score: 100/100
   - Load Time: ~0.09 seconds
   - Fast and responsive

2. **Perfect Accessibility** ✅
   - Accessibility Score: 100/100
   - WCAG compliant
   - Important for business positioning

3. **Good Security** ✅
   - Proper HTTPS
   - Security headers configured
   - Safe for visitors
